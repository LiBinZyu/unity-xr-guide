# XR Technology Overview

XR (Extended Reality) extended reality technology is the collective term for virtual reality, augmented reality, and mixed reality.

## üì± Technology Classification

### VR - Virtual Reality
Fully immersive digital environment experience.

**Characteristics:**
- Completely virtual environment
- 360-degree field of view
- Strong immersion
- Isolation from real world

**Typical Devices:**
- HTC Vive
- Oculus Quest
- Valve Index
- PlayStation VR

### AR - Augmented Reality
Overlaying digital information on top of the real world.

**Characteristics:**
- Preserves real environment
- Digital content merges with reality
- Real-time interaction
- High mobility

**Typical Devices:**
- iPhone/iPad (ARKit)
- Android phones (ARCore)
- Microsoft HoloLens
- Magic Leap

### MR - Mixed Reality
Deep integration of VR and AR.

**Characteristics:**
- More natural fusion of virtual and real
- Digital objects understand physical environment
- More complex interaction methods
- Higher technical requirements
